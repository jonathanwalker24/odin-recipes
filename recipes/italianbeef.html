Italian Beef

Description

This is an easy recipe that can feed the family for days. It is made crock pot and is very easy. It requires few ingredients yet is bursting with flavor.

Ingedients
  • 3-4 pound chuck roast
  • 1 packet italian dressing mix
  • 1 jar pepperoncini

Steps
  1. Set up crockpot on low heat.
  2. Trim fat from roast and set in pot.
  3. Pour italian seasoning on top.
  4. Pour jar of pepperoncini on top.
  5. Add water until roast is almost covered.
  6. Cover and cook on low for 12 hrs.
  7. Shred roast and serve on toasted sandwich rolls.
